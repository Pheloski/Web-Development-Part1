1. Organisation Overview

Name: Mama B Bakes

Mama B Bakes is a homegrown online bakery established in 2021 by Mama B, a passionate baker who turned her love for homemade treats into a thriving small business. Based in Midrand, the bakery began by serving friends and family before expanding through social media and word of mouth. Today, it offers a variety of baked goods, including cakes, biscuits, cupcakes, and custom celebration treats.

**Mission:**
To deliver high-quality, fresh, home-baked goods that bring comfort and joy to every customer.

*Vision:**
To become a trusted and well-known bakery brand in Johannesburg and beyond, recognized for creativity, quality, and excellent customer service.

**Target Audience:**

* Families
* Young professionals
* Event planners
* Individuals in Midrand and surrounding areas seeking delicious, convenient, and custom baked goods

---

## 2. Website Goals and Objectives

**Goal:**
To create a user-friendly online platform that boosts sales and customer engagement.

**Objectives:**

* Showcase custom cake designs
* Allow customers to browse and order products online
* Increase customer inquiries and orders

**Key Performance Indicators (KPIs):**

* Number of online orders
* Customer engagement levels
* Monthly website traffic

---

## 3. Current Website Analysis

Mama B Bakes currently does not have a website and relies on social media platforms, which limits accessibility.

**Strengths:**

* Existing customer base
* Strong visual content

**Weaknesses:**

* Limited online business credibility
* No central ordering system

**Improvements Needed:**

* Streamlined ordering process
* Dedicated website to strengthen brand identity
